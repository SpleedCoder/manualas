Information about public services and notable places are available here.

Government & Public Services

Zou Environmental Protection Bureau

Hotline: (02) 8283-1234

Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Closed: Weekends and holidays

Services: Responsible for protecting the natural environment Zou. Offers waste management, tree conservation, and environmental awareness programs.

Wano Cultural Heritage Office

Hotline: (02) 8374-4567

Office Hours: Tuesday-Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM Closed: Sundays and Mondays

Closed: Weekends and holidays

Services: Promotes cultural heritage preservation. Hosts events, workshops, and exhibitions showcasing Wano's traditions and history.

Skypiea Emergency Response Team (SERT)

Hotline: (02) 8911-0069

Office Hours (Admin & Non-Emergency Services): Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Emergency Response: 24/7

Closed: Weekends and holidays

Services: Provides emergency medical assistance, fire response, and disaster relief services within Skypiea and neighboring sitios.

Manualas Education and Learning Center (MELC)

Hotline: (02) 8256-0001

Office Hours: Monday-Friday, 7:30 AM - 4:30 PM Closed: Weekends and public holidays

Closed: Weekends and holidays

Services: Oversees primary and secondary schools, offers educational resources, and conducts literacy programs.

Elbaf Manualas Health and Wellness Clinic

Hotline: (02) 8345-9999

Office Hours: Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM Emergency Services: 24/7 for urgent medical cases

Services: Provides medical consultations, vaccination programs, mental health support, and maternal care.

For further assistance or inquiries, residents and visitors can contact the Barangay Manualas Unified Hotline at (02) 8888-0000, available 24/7.

Notable Places

Brgy. Manualas Barangay Hall

This is the barangay hall of Brgy. Manualas. If you have concerns, you may approach the barangay officials or the tanods.


Barangay Hall Address: 836 Talisay Ave. Tierra Subd. Manualas, Elbaf City

Manualas Fire Department

The Manualas Fire Department is always ready to respond to emergencies and keep the community safe. They also conduct fire safety programs to teach people how to stay safe in case of a fire.


Location: Munting Lupa Lane, close to Zou Environmental Protection Bureau

Zou Environmental Protection Bureau

The bureau protects Manualas and Zou's environment by managing waste, conserving trees, wnd teaching people how to care for nature. ZEPB works to keep the community clean and healthy environment.


Location: Munting Lupa Lane, close to Manualas Fire Department

Elbaf Manualas Health and Wellness Clinic

The Elbaf Manualas Health and Wellness Clinic provides comprehensive healthcare services, including medical consultations, vaccinations, mental health support, and maternal care to residents of Manualas.


Location: Between Datu Ezel Street & Hiraya Street, close to Manualas Fire Department

Manualas Education and Learning Center (MELC)

MELC helps kids and teens in Manualas learn and grow by providing school resources and support. It also offers programs to help people of all ages improve their reading and writing skills.


Location: Along Haranah Road, close to Elbaf National Library

Manualas High School

Manualas High School offers Junior high and Senior high with a focus on academic excellence and personal growth. It provides various extracurricular activities to support student development.


Location: At Salimbay Street, close to Manualas Grand Plaza

Manualas Paws & Play Park

The best place for pets and their owners, the Manualas Paws & Play Park is a fun space with obstacle courses, open fields, and playground. It's a great place to bond with your furry friends and meet fellow pet lovers.


Location: Bagwis Boulevard, close to Manualas Sports Complex

Manualas Sports Complex

The Manualas Sports Complex is where athletes and sports fans gather and where competitions are held. It has basketball courts and a gym, making it the best place to practice, compete, or cheer for your favorite teams.


Location: Bagwis Boulevard, close to Manualas Paws & Play Park

Elbaf National Libary

A quiet and peaceful place, the Elbaf National Library offers tons of books and digital resources for students and learners. Whether you're doing research or just reading for fun, it's a great spot to focus and learn.


Location: Along Haranah Road, close to Manualas Education and Learning Center (MELC)

Manualas Central Park

This park is the go-to spot for relaxing walks family picnis, and fun activities. With its green spaces and play areas, Manualas Central Park is perfect for spending a chill day with loved ones.


Location: Elbaf Boulevard

Manualas Grand Plaza

Manualas Grand Plaza is a popular spot where people gather for community events, celebrations, and festivals. It's a great place to hang out, enjoy the outdoors, and catch up with friends.


Location: Malaya Street, close to Manualas High School

Manualas Elementary School

Manualas Elementary School provides quality primary education for young learners. It offers a nurturing environment where students can learn and build a strong academic foundation.


Location: Banahaw Subd.
